Help & Support

Your questions answered. Your journey made easier.

How do I generate a worksheet?

Click on "Generate Worksheet" in the navigation bar. Fill in your subject and level, then let the AI do its magic!

Do I need an account to use MagicSheets.ai?

You can try out basic generation without an account, but saving, editing, and accessing community sheets requires sign-up.

Can I download my worksheets?

Absolutely! Every worksheet comes with a download option in PDF format.

What are Community Sheets?

These are shared worksheets from other educators. You can like, remix, or save them for later use.

Can I customize worksheet themes?

Yes! Premium users can choose from multiple themes and add personal touches before downloading or sharing.
